The Magic of Thinking Big by David Schwartz, Ph.D.

Get the Book on Amazon.

Core Principle: Promotes the power of positive thinking and aiming for ambitious goals to achieve greater success and fulfillment in life.

    Key Lessons:
  • Believe You Can Succeed and You Will: Schwartz emphasizes that developing a strong self-belief and a positive mental attitude are crucial for achieving your goals.
  • Stop Making Excuses: The book encourages taking responsibility for your life and overcoming self-limiting beliefs that hold you back.
  • Build Confidence and Destroy Fear: Schwartz offers strategies for conquering fear and building unshakeable confidence in your abilities.
  • Think and Dream Creatively: The book promotes the importance of creative thinking and problem-solving to find innovative solutions and achieve your goals.
  • Look Important - It Helps You Think Important: Schwartz suggests that carrying yourself with confidence and dressing for success can positively impact your mindset and self-belief.
  • Manage Your Environment: Create a physical and social environment that supports your goals and positive thinking.
  • Make Your Attitudes Your Allies: Schwartz emphasizes the power of positive self-talk and cultivating an optimistic outlook.
  • Think Right Toward People: The book encourages building strong relationships and treating others with respect.
  • Get the Action Habit: Taking action and developing a "go-getter" mentality are essential for turning dreams into reality.
  • Turn Defeat Into Victory: Schwartz teaches valuable strategies for learning from setbacks and bouncing back from challenges.
  • Use Goals To Continue To Grow: Setting ambitious goals and continuously striving for improvement fuels personal growth and success.
  • How to Think Like A Leader: The book encourages developing a leadership mindset, taking initiative, and inspiring others.
