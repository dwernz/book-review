Core Principle:The Go-Giver Influencer expands on the core principle of the Go-Giver series - success comes from focusing on giving value and building genuine relationships - but emphasizes the art of influence in today's world.

    Key Lessons:
  • The Five Secrets of Genuine Influence: The book introduces five key principles for becoming a more influential person:
    • Breathe. Master Your Emotions. Set your feelings aside. You can still have your feelings; you don't even need to change them. Just set them aside for the moment. Don't let them drive the car. Put reasoned judgement in the driver's seat, feeling in the passenger seat. Retrain yourself to respond to conflict and disagreement by unruffling your feelings. Make calm your default setting.
    • Listen. Step Into the Other Person's Shoes. Get out of your own head and step into the other person's perspective. See the world through their lenses. Appreciate where they're coming from and what's at stake for them. Listen with the back of your neck.
    • Smile. Set the Frame. Take the initiative to establish the tone and context of the interaction. Whoever sets the frame of the conversation also sets the direction and tone in which it will go.
    • Be Gracious. Communicate with Tact and Empathy. Let yourself feel what the other person is feeling, and speak to that truthfully, yet also with compassion. No matter how different they may seem or what position they may take, remember that they are a chime and you are a tuning fork.
    • Trust. Let Go of Having to be Right. As long as your premise is that your position is the right one, and the other person's is the wrong one, you have no chance of arriving at a genuinely satisfying solution. Sometimes you have to let go, and eat the pancakes. And surprise! You may find you have what you let go of.

  • The Parable: The story follows two ambitious young professionals, Gillian (a buyer) and Jackson (a manufacturer), who each want something from the other. Through encounters with a wise mentor, they learn the power of giving value and influencing through genuine connection, ultimately achieving success beyond their initial goals.
  • Shifting the focus: The Go-Giver Influencer goes beyond just closing deals or making sales. It emphasizes building trust, long-term relationships, and creating win-win situations for everyone involved.
